What is E.D.G.E.?

More Than Just a Competition

E.D.G.E. (Explore Design Gear-up Exhibit) is the annual flagship mini-project competition hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Applications. It's a platform designed to ignite the spark of innovation among our UG and PG students.

We challenge you to push the boundaries of technology, transform your theoretical knowledge into practical, running projects, and present your solutions to a panel of experts. Itâ€™s your chance to shine, get recognized, and prove that you have the edge.

Whether you're passionate about AI, securing the cloud, building the next killer app, or exploring the decentralized world of blockchain, E.D.G.E. has a track for you.

Rules & Regulations

Play Fair, Code Hard

Eligibility

Open to all Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG) students of the Department of Computer Applications, MIT-WPU.

Team Size

A maximum of 3 members are allowed per team. You can also participate individually.

Project Requirement

Only fully functional, running projects will be accepted for evaluation. Be prepared to demonstrate your project live.

Track Viability

A competition track may be dropped if fewer than two groups are registered for it.
